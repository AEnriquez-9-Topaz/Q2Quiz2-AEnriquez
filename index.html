Welcome to OBGH Computer Club!!


Welcome to OB Montessori Greenhills' Robotics Club, where creativity and innovation are brought to life! Our group is committed to creating and inventing devices that not only enable us to solve problems in the real world but also provide us with opportunities to participate in a variety of robotics challenges and contests. Our members have the exceptional chance to learn about the most recent scientific and technological advancements while exploring the intriguing field of quantum engineering. We believe in providing a hands-on, collaborative environment where both beginners and seasoned specialists may learn, grow, and push the boundaries of what is possible. Whether you have a strong interest in robotics, are keen to pick up new abilities, or are thrilled to be a part of an innovative community, the Robotics Club at OB Montessori Greenhills is the perfect!


The Robotics Club at OB Montessori Greenhills has a rich history rooted in passion, innovation, and collaboration. Since its inception, the club has been dedicated to fostering creativity and technical skills among students, providing them with the opportunity to explore the exciting world of robotics. Over the years, the club has participated in numerous local and national competitions, steadily growing its reputation for excellence. With each challenge, members have refined their skills, worked on complex projects, and earned recognition for their ingenuity. Today, the club stands as a powerhouse of innovation, with a string of recent award wins that reflect the hard work and dedication of our talented members.
